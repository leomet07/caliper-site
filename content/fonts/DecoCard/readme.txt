DECO CARDA true type font by P.D. Magnuswww.fontmonkey.comDeco Card is an art deco-ish font with glyphs for letters, numbers, anda good deal of punctuation (including the Euro symbol).

It was designed in PfaEdit and reworked in Type Tool.This font is copyright 2002-4 by P.D. Magnus. `Deco card', 'FontMonkey', and 'fecundity.com' are trademarks of P.D. Magnus.
